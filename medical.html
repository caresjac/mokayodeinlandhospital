Surgery

Surgery refers to medical procedures involving incisions or manipulation of the body to diagnose, treat, or prevent various conditions. It encompasses a broad range of interventions, from routine outpatient surgeries to complex, life-saving procedures. Surgical care involves preoperative preparations, the surgical procedure itself, and postoperative recovery.

The surgical process involves preoperative preparation, the actual surgery, and postoperative care. Recent advancements include robot-assisted surgery, telemedicine applications, and improved anesthesia techniques, enhancing the precision and safety of surgical interventions. Surgery remains a crucial component of modern medicine, addressing a wide range of health issues and improving patient outcomes.
