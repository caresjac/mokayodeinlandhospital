Senior In-Home Care Services

Given the choice, most of us want to stay in our homes. Sometimes, people need help to remain at home. That’s where Mokayode Inland Hospital comes in.

OUR MISSION

Helping Seniors Age Successfully Safe at Home

Put simply, it is our mission to improve the quality of life for seniors and adults with disabilities. Of course, we recognize that everyone’s idea of “quality of life” is different, which is why it’s so important to us to make you an active part of your own care plan. We want you to be in the driver’s seat regardless of any limitations or challenges you’re facing.
